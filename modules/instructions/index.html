Baccarat (Punto Banco) Rules and Procedures

1. Basic Rules and Objective

Baccarat is a comparing card game played between two hands: Player (Punto) and Banker (Banco). The objective is to bet on which hand will have a total closest to 9.

Possible Outcomes

  • Player hand wins
  • Banker hand wins
  • Tie (Egalité)

Game Flow

  1. Players place bets on Player, Banker, or Tie
  2. Two cards are dealt to both Player and Banker hands
  3. Based on specific rules, a third card may be drawn
  4. Hand closest to 9 wins the round

All third-card decisions are automatic based on fixed rules. Players only need to place their initial bets.

2. Card Values and Scoring

Number Cards (2-9)

Worth face value

10s and Face Cards

Worth 0 points

10, J, Q, K = 0

Aces

Worth 1 point

Scoring Rules

  • Only the rightmost digit of the total is used
  • Double-digit totals drop the first digit

Scoring Examples

  • 7 + 5 = 12 → 2 points
  • 9 + K = 9 points (King = 0)
  • 4 + 6 = 10 → 0 points

4. Game Procedures

Round Sequence

  1. Betting phase
    • Players place wagers
    • Dealer announces "no more bets"
  2. Initial deal
    • Two cards to Player and Banker
  3. Natural check
    • Check for 8 or 9 totals
  4. Third card phase (if needed)
    • Follow drawing rules
  5. Resolution
    • Compare finals totals
    • Process payouts and collect losses

5. Third Card Rules

Player Hand Rules

  • Totals 0-5: Must draw
  • Totals 6-7: Must stand
  • Totals 8-9: Natural - no draw

Banker Hand Rules

When Player Stands (No Third Card)

  • Totals 0-5: Must draw
  • Totals 6-7: Must stand
  • Totals 8-9: Natural - no draw

When Player Draws Third Card

Banker Total 0, 1, or 2: Always draws

Banker Total 3: Draws on Player's third card 0-7, 9; Stands on 8

Banker Total 4: Draws on Player's third card 2-7; Stands on 0-1, 8-9

Banker Total 5: Draws on Player's third card 4-7; Stands on 0-3, 8-9

Banker Total 6: Draws on Player's third card 6-7; Stands on 0-5, 8-9

Banker Total 7: Always stands

6. Payouts and Commissions

Player Bet

1 to 1 (Even money)

Bet $100 → Win $100

Banker Bet

0.95 to 1 (5% commission paid at time of win)

Bet $100 → Win $95

Tie Bet

8 to 1 or 9 to 1 (check house rules)

Bet $100 → Win $800 or $900

7. Dealer Responsibilities

Primary Duties

  1. Bet management
    • Monitor and verify bet placements
    • Ensure minimum/maximum compliance
  2. Card handling
    • Deal according to fixed rules
    • Maintain card security
  3. Financial transactions
    • Calculate and collect commissions at time of win
    • Process accurate payouts
  4. Game integrity
    • Maintain proper etiquette
    • Follow security protocols
    • Apply house rules consistently

These rules describe Punto Banco, the standard version of Baccarat played in North America.
